Focaccia with caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN
Fresh Cranberry recpies That Go Beyond Sauce

 

Ingredients

For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all purpose flous
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan
For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a pairing knife if desire, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cup fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flous is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl by mixing byu hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
